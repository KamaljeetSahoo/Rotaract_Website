SaPNA

"We make a living by what we get, but we make a life by what we give." The members of the Rotaract Club of NIT Rourkela, visited OSAP Battalion, sector-2 on 24th of March, to organize a Menstrual Awareness Campaign. The event was themed, SaPNA: Sanitary Pads 'n Awareness. The money collected during fundraising events like Housie and V'day are reimbursed in this event. The prime purpose was setting up platforms that encourage discussions about menstruation and, raise awareness amongst the slum women on the necessity of using sanitary pads. The atmosphere was eased with the screening of the movie "Padman". The ladies of different age groups gathered and they were told about the safety precautions that they should uptake during menstruation. There was a door to door sanitary pads distribution. The happiness on their face, after the interactive session, was our biggest reward.

The Rotaract family hopes to bring joy to each and every one.

LOVE BOOTH

We had our pre-valentine event "love booth", where we provided a sky of fun to the love birds. Valentines Day Card was sold by Rotaract Club of NIT Rourkela during NITRUtsav and the money collected by selling them, were used to conduct other events. V-day is the major fundraiser project for the club. In this project, club members make handmade as well as printed cards and these are sold among the mass. The v-day project was a very successful event of Rotaract club. This year Rotaractors sold numerous cards and managed to collect very good fund. Club also provided to facility to deliver card at recipients room on Valentine's day and sending card anonymously was the best idea which helped in increasing the sales. The club sold printed cards for Valentine, friend as well as for prank in three different sizes.

Housie Event

The Rotaract Club organized a fun event, "Tambola" in the institute where we sold a large number of housie tickets and the money raised by selling the tickets are used for a good cause. The event took place on 4th February at the Lecture Avenue of the institute where a large mass of enthusiastic students was there to be a part of the event.The event gathered a huge crowd as people kept coming in, to try out their luck. Each ticket cost 10 rupees. The money raised from the housie and VDay , was utilized to organize an event "SaPNA", awareness on menstruation, at the OSAP Battalion slum. The happiness that we see in the students' eyes is what we work for and that happiness is what satisfies us.
